Help Center

Use this guide to understand how quote calculations, account access, booking workflows, and policy terms work across the Freight Services portal. A self-service workflow guide is included below for rapid quote-to-booking actions.

Purpose

The FSI Self-Service Quote Tool is designed to help customers obtain rapid shipment pricing and proceed to booking without waiting for manual quote turnaround.

Supported Service Types

  • Domestic door-to-door next day air freight.
  • Direct point-to-point hotshot cargo runs.

Required Inputs

  1. Origin ZIP code
  2. Destination ZIP code
  3. Total weight
  4. Number of pieces
  5. Service type (Hotshot or Next Day Air)
  6. Any required accessorial services (for example, liftgate, residential delivery, limited access)
Important: Accessorial selections impact the final total. If required services are omitted during quote entry, rates may increase during final shipment review.
{% if is_internal %}
Internal technical notes: Accessorial logic and ZIP validation are implemented by the quote services layer. Ensure new accessorial codes are documented for customer users when added to admin rate tables.
{% endif %}

Quote Result Breakdown

After you submit a quote request, review the following fields before continuing:

  • Base Charge: core transportation rate based on service, distance, and shipment details.
  • Accessorials: itemized costs for selected specialized services.
  • Total Miles: estimated route distance used for quote generation.
  • Warnings: highlighted notices when shipment details exceed standard thresholds.

Always validate that the quote matches shipment requirements before selecting a booking pathway.

Booking and Sharing Options

  1. Book Directly via TS2000: Select Book Quote to continue in the Freight Services TransSoft portal.
  2. Email for Operations Booking: Select Email to Request Booking to have operations finalize the load.
  3. Save/Share Quote: Use Send to Myself for your own copy, or Email this quote summary (staff-level accounts) to send customer-ready details.
Operations booking fee: A $15 administrative fee applies when operations finalizes booking from email requests.

Important Disclaimers

  • All automated quotes are subject to final evaluation and approval by Freight Services Inc.
  • Rates may increase when shipment requirements differ from original quote inputs, including missing accessorial services.

Overview

The FSI self-service quote workflow helps customers rapidly price shipments and move directly to booking.

  • Domestic door-to-door next day air freight.
  • Direct point-to-point hotshot cargo runs.

Generating a Quote

Enter origin and destination ZIP codes, shipment weight, piece count, service level, and any required accessorials.

Important: Accessorial selections affect total charges. Missing required services may result in rate changes during final review.

Reviewing Results

  • Base Charge: Core transportation rate.
  • Accessorials: Itemized specialized service charges.
  • Total Miles: Estimated shipment distance.
  • Warnings: Highlighted notices when thresholds are exceeded.

Managing and Finalizing Shipments

  1. Book Directly via TS2000: Use Book Quote to continue in the Freight Services TransSoft portal.
  2. Email for Operations Booking: Use Email to Request Booking to have operations finalize shipment entry.
  3. Save and Share: Use Send to Myself for your receipt, or Email this quote summary for staff-driven customer updates.
Operations booking fee: A $15 administrative fee applies to bookings finalized by operations.

Important Disclaimers

  • All automated quotes are subject to final review and approval by Freight Services Inc.
  • Rates may increase when final shipment requirements differ from quote inputs.
